Cold Curried Pea Soup

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 1/3 cup dried green or yellow split peas, sorted and soaked
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
    B
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
    C
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper, ground

Method

Cook A about 1 hour in about 1 ½ times its volume in water. Partially mash when tender. Microwave B 4 minutes. Add. Add C and cook another 30 minutes. Chill.
