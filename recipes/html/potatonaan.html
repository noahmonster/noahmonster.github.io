Potato Naan

Ingredients

    A
  • 4-5 cups flour
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp (½ pkg) yeast
  • 3 Tbs yogurt
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup oil
  • optional: minced garlic
  • 1 cup warm water
    B
  • 1 large potato
    C
  • ½ onion, chopped and fried golden
  • salt
  • milk or half and half

Method

Knead A together into soft dough and allow to rise in a warm place until doubled, about 1-1½ hours.

While dough is rising, microwave or bake B, then mash coarsely with C.

Roll the dough into a long rope. Divide into eight pieces and place into a floured, covered container. One at a time, do the following:

Roll each piece into a ball, then, using lots of flour on the counter, roll out into a circle about 5" in diameter. Put a tablespoon of potato in the center, then fold over and seal up the edges well. Turn the sealed edges under and roll into a 6-7" circle, trying to even out the distribution of potato across the whole circle. Make four of these, then:

Bake 2½ minutes per side on tiles in a preheated oven, 500 °F
