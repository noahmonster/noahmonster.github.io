Baked Custard Rice Pudding

Ingredients

    A
  • 6 eggs
    B
  • ¾ cup sugar
    C
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ¾ cup raisins
  • 2 ½ cups milk
    D
  • ground cinnamon

Method

Beat A. Beat in B. Mix in C. pour into buttered casserole. Place casserole in a pan in a 350° oven and pour about ½ inch of boiling water into the pan. Bake about 30 minutes until semi-firm. Stir once or twice during cooking to distribute the raisins and rice.
