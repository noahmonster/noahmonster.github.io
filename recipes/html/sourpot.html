Sourdough Potato Rolls

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 medium potatoes, cooked and mashed
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1½ cup water
  • 1 cup flour
    B
  • 1 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 Tbs sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 Tbs salt
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 3-4 cups flour

Method

Mix A together, stirring vigorously for 100 strokes. Cover loosely and allow to ferment for 12-36 hours.

Combine A and B to make a soft dough and knead. Cover with moist towel and allow to rise double in the proverbial warm place.

Roll the dough out into a long rope and divide into 12-24 pieces. Hand form each into a roll, and place on either a baking sheet or (preferably) a sheet of parchment paper. Cover with moist towel and allow to rise double in that warm place.

Slide the parchment sheet onto the stones of a 400° oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, depending on size.
