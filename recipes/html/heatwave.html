Pineapple Curry Heat Wave

Ingredients

  • 12 habanero peppers
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 1 cup canned pineapple chunks
  • 4 Tbs pineapple juice from can
  • 3 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp mustard powder
  • 1 tsp coriander seed
  • 1 tsp celery seed
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp cayenne
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 4 Tbs vinegar
  • 2 Tbs honey
  • ½ cup water

Method

Warning: wear gloves.

Purée all in blender. Simmer 10 min. Transfer to jars and freeze.
