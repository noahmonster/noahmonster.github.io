Matzo Kugel

Ingredients

    A
  • whites of 8 eggs
    B
  • yolks of 8 eggs
    C
  • 8 maztzos, soak in water and squeeze out excess
  • 2 Tbs lemon jice
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ cup oil
  • 4 apples, chopped
  • 2 onions, chopped and stir-fried

Method

Beat A to stiff-peak stage. Reserve. Beat B. Mix in everything else. Fold in A. Bake in two greased loaf pans ~ 1 hr @ 350°.
