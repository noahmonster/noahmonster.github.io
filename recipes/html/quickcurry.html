Quick Chicken and Noodles in Curry Sauce

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 pkg egg noodles
    B
  • 2 skinned, boned chicken breasts (4 halves)
  • 3 ¼ cups water
  • ¼ cup white wine
  • 1 bay leaf
  • pepper
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • ¼ tsp dried thyme
    C
  • 6 Tbs flour
  • 2 Tbs curry powder
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • 1/8 tsp habanero pepper or ¼ tsp cayenne

Method

Boil a large pot of water for A, and cook these when there's a lull in the remaining procedures.

Simmer B 10 minutes. Reserve 2 cups of the liquid. Chop the breasts into chunks and reserve.

Make a gravy out of C and the reserved liquid from B.

Combine meat and gravy and serve over buttered (optional) noodles.
