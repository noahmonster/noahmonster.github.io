Chocolate Fudge Cake

Ingredients

A E
4 oz unsweetened baking chocolate ¼ cup oil or butter
1 ½ cups sugar
B
2 egg whites F
1 ¾ cups flour
C 2 tsp baking powder
½ cup sugar ½ tsp salt
   
D G
2 egg yolks 1 ½ cups milk
2 tsp vanilla

Method

Preheat oven to 350°.

Prepare two 8" cake pans or one 8" x 12" sheet pan as follows: grease or butter the pans, line bottom with parchment or foil to aid removal, then grease the lining. Coat the inside of the pans with flour and shake out the excess.

Melt A gently in microwave and reserve. Beat B to stiff peak stage. Add C and beat a little more. Reserve. Beat D until lemon colored. Reserve. Beat E together in a large bowl. Beat in D. Beat in A. Alternately beat in a little of each, F and G, until all is combined. Fold in A.

Fill the pans, then bake about 35 minutes (50 minutes for a sheet cake), then test with a toothpick every 5 minutes. Remove when middle is just done (when cake stops sticking to toothpick).

Remove from pans after 10 minutes. When cool cover with Chocolate Frosting.
