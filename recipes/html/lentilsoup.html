Curried Lentil Soup

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 pkg (2 cups) lentils, preferably red, sorted and soaked
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • 2 cloves, ground
  • ½ tsp cumin, ground
  • ¼ tsp cardamom seeds, ground
  • ½ tsp mustard seed, ground
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ¼ tsp cayenne
    B
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
    C
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup milk

Method

Prepare A: cover with 1 ½ times water, bring to a boil and turn down to a simmer. Cook about 45 minutes. Blend if possible, or mash. Microwave B 3 minutes, then add to lentils. Add C. Cook another 15 minutes.
