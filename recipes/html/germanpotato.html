German Potato Salad

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 onion, sliced and soaked in ¼ cup vinegar
    B
  • 3 cups sliced cooked red potatoes (see Method)
    C
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • 1 Tbs flour
  • 1 Tbs sugar
  • 3 Tbs water
    D
  • ½ tsp celery seed
  • pepper
  • ½ tsp salt

Method

Set A to soaking.

Peel and slice B, boil for ~8 minutes until done. Drain and reserve

Combine C into a paste.

Bring A, C, and D to a boil. Pour over B. Mix and chill.
