How to Thicken With Cornstarch

Cornstarch is a quick thickener for stews, but must be added properly. If you just dump it in dry you will wind up with big gummy lumps.

Put the cornstarch in a glass or bowl and add ¼-½ cup cold water while stirring, until it is completely lump free and looks like milk. Stir this into the hot stew and continue stirring until it thickens.
