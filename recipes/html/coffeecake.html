Quick Coffee Cake

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ cup sugar
    B
  • 1 fork-beaten egg
  • ½ cup milk
  • 3 Tbs oil

Method

Sift A and mix together. Combine B, then stir into A. Spoon batter into greased 8" cake pan or flat casserole dish. Sprinkle top with a little oil, sugar, and cinnamon. Bake ~ 25 minutes @ 375°. Done when a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.
