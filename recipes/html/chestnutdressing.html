Thanksgiving Chestnut Dressing

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • ¼ cup diced celery
    B
  • 3 potatoes, diced
  • ¼ lb sausage meat
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cups chestnuts, boiled and peeled
    C
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of bread, soaked in stock or water
  • pepper and salt

    Method

    Stir-fry A. Add B and continue to stir-fry until meat is cooked. Remove from heat. Combine C in a bowl and mix in A/B. Bake in greased bowl 1 ¼ hr @ 350°.

    Alternative: Stuff turkey with this mixture. Center of stuffing must reach 160°.
