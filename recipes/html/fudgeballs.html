Fudge Balls

Ingredients

  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 squares unsweetened baking chocolate
  • candy sprinkles

Method

Stir in in double boiler until as thick as possible (alternatively stir in heavy-bottomed saucepan over low heat — stir constantly so as not to scorch).

Cool in refrigerator. Scoop into balls and roll in sprinkles.
