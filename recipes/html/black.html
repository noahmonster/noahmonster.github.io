Black Bean Soup

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 cups dried black beans, cooked in enough water to cover double.
    B
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
    C
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes
  • 2 Tbs soy sauce
    D
  • 2 cups frozen corn
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage

Method

Prepare A. Stir-fry B, or microwave 2 minutes. Add to beans. Add C and simmer 20 minutes. Add D and simmer 10 minutes.
