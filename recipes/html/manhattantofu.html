Manhattan Tofu Chowder

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/3 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
    B
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 medium potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • black pepper, ground
  • 1 bay leaf (warning: do not eat)
  • ¼ tsp ground thyme
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1¼ cup water
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes
    C
  • tofu cutlets, cubed
  • 1 cup frozen corn

Method

Sir-fry A, or microwave 2 minutes. Add to B and simmer 20 minutes, covered. Add C and cook 5 minutes.
