Vegetable Curry in Pea Sauce

Ingredients

    A
  • ½ cup yellow split peas, cooked fairly dry and mashed.
    B
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 1 ½ Tbs minced ginger
    C
  • 1 tsp black mustard seeds
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tsp cumin, ground
  • 2 tsp coriander, ground
    D
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes
    E
  • 1 cauliflower, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, sliced
    F
  • 1 eggplant, peeled and chopped
    G
  • mushrooms, sliced

Method

Prepare A and reserve. Stir-fry B until onions are translucent, then add C briefly. Add D and simmer 10 minutes. Add E and simmer 10 minutes. Add F and simmer 10 minutes. Add G and cook 5 minutes. Add A.
