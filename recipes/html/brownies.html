Brownies

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 oz. unsweetened baking chocolate
    B
  • ¼ cup oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 lightly beaten eggs
  • 2 Tbs milk
    C
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
    D
  • 1 cup walnuts (optional)

Method

Melt A in microwave. Beat B with a fork. Sift C. Combine everything. Pour into buttered baking dish and bake 30 minutes @ 325°. Done when a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.
