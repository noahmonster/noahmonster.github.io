Gazpacho

Ingredients

  • 2 cans tomato bisque soup
  • 2 cans water
  • 1 envelope vegetable soup/dip mix
  • ¼ cup vinegar
  • 1 Tbs oil
  • 1 tsp worcesershire sauce
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 diced green pepper
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 cup frozen or fresh corn
  • 1 15-oz. can diced tomatoes

Method

Combine. chill. Serves 8
