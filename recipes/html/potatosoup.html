Indian Potato Soup

Ingredients

    A
  • 4 cups water
    B
  • ½ can tomato paste
    C
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and quartered
    D
  • ¼ tsp cumin seed, ground
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp dried ground habanero

Method

Boil all 45 min. Crush the potatoes with a ricer or stick blender. Serve with some minced cilantro.
