Lemon Poppy Bread

Ingredients

    A
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1½ cup white flour
  • ¼ cup whole-wheat flour
  • ½ tsp yeast
    B
  • 3 Tbs poppy seeds
  • ¼ cup honey
  • grated zest of 1 lemon
  • 1½ cups whole-wheat flour
  • ~4 cups white flour
  • 1 Tbs salt
  • ½ tsp yeast
    C
  • 2 cups warm water

Method

Combine A in a large bowl and stir 100 strokes. Cover this sponge with a plate and allow to ferment overnight. Add B to A. Add C and knead into soft dough, adding more white flour as needed. Rise double. Divide into two loaves. Cover with damp towel and rise double. Bake in pans or freeform, 20-30 minutes @ 400 °F.
