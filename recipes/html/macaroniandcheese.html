Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients

    A
  • 8 oz (2 cups) macaroni
    B
  • 6 Tbs flour
  • 2 ½ cups milk
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • pepper
    C
  • 4 oz sharp cheddar, cubed
  • 4 oz sharp cheddar, sliced
    D
  • Tomato slices (optional)

Method

Boil A until firm but not hard. Drain.

Make sauce from B: Stir a little milk into flour and spices to make a paste — keep adding milk until you can dump it all in without making lumps. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring constantly to avoid scorching bottom. When thickened, remove 1 ¼ cup. This will be mixed into the macaroni. What's left in the pot will be the topping: add the sliced cheese and allow to heat slowly to melt.

Pour the removed 1 ¼ cup sauce onto the macaroni, add the cubed cheese, and stir. Pour into the casserole dish.

Stir the pot of cheese topping until the cheese is completely dissolved. Pour onto the macaroni casserole.

Bake covered 25 minutes @ 325°. Uncover and bake another 15-20 minutes until bubbly and lightly browned across the top.
