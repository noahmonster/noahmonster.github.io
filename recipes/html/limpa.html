Swedish Limpa

Ingredients (three loaves – process in two batches)

(need two batches of this)
  • 2 Tbs honey
  • ¼ tsp ground cardamom
  • 1 Tbs caraway seed
  • 1 Tbs salt
  • 2 tsp yeast
  • 2 ½ cups rye flour
  • 3-3 ½ cups white flour
  • 2 cups beer

Method

Knead in two batches, then combine). Cover with damp towel. Rise in a warm place until doubled. Divide into three, fold into loaves. Rise in lightly greased pans or on parchment until doubled. Bake 20 min @ 400°. Done when lightly browned. Pop out of pan and rap on bottom — if not hollow sounding, put loaf directly on rack and bake another 5 min, or more.
