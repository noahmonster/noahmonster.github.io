Aunt Janis' Famous Pumpkin Bread

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 2/3 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ¾ tsp salt
    B
  • 1/8 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • ½ cup raisins
    C
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ¼ cup oil
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin

Method

Sift A, add B, and mix together. Beat C, then stir in A and B. Bake in greased loaf pan ~ 1 hour @ 325°. Done when a skewer pushed into the center comes out clean.
