Vegetable Curry

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 green peppers, sliced
  • 1 slice ginger
    B
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups water or stock
  • 1 Tbs curry powder
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp cayenne
  • ¼ tsp ground thyme
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
    C
  • 1 eggplant, peeled, sliced and the slices quartered.
  • 1 cauliflower, small chunks
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and diced
    D
  • 2 turnips, peeled and diced
  • 2 zucchini, sliced
    E
  • scallions or mushrooms or peas

Method

Stir-fry A. Add B and simmer 20 minutes. Add C and simmer 10 minutes. Add D and simmer 5 minutes. Add E and simmer 5 minutes. Thicken with 2 Tbs cornstarch if desired.
