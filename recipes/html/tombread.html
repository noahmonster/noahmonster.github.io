Tomato Bread

Ingredients (two loaves)

    A
  • ½ cup warm water
  • ¾ cup white flour
  • ½ tsp yeast
    B
  • 3.5 oz. pkg sun-dried (not in oil) julienne tomatoes
  • 1 cup whole-wheat flour
  • 4-5 cups white flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp yeast
    C
  • 2 cups warm water

Ingredients (three loaves)

    A – divide in half after rising
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 1 1/8 cup white flour (150grams)
  • 1 tsp yeast
    B – need two batches of this
  • 1 pkg sun-dried (not in oil) julienne tomatoes
  • 1 cup whole-wheat flour (150 grams)
  • 4-5 cups white flour (525 grams)
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • Half of A
    C – need two of this
  • 1 7/8 cups warm water

Method

Two loaves (one batch)

Combine A in a large bowl and stir 100 strokes. Cover this sponge with a plate and allow to ferment overnight. Add A to B. Add C and knead into soft dough, adding more white flour as needed. Rise double. Divide into two loaves. Cover with damp towel and rise double. Bake in pans or freeform, 20-30 minutes @ 400 °F.

Three loaves (two batches)

After fermenting the sponge overnight, divide it in two. Follow the instructions above to make two batches of dough. Combine the batches for the first rising, then divide into three loaves for the second rising and the baking.
