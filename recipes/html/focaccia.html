Focaccia

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 ¾ cup warm water
  • 3½ - 4½ cups white flour
  • ¼ cup whole-wheat flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp yeast
    B
  • 1 red onion, sliced thinly
  • kosher salt
  • grated parmesan or romano cheese

Method

Combine A and knead for 15 minutes. Dough should be very soft. Cover and refrigerate overnight.

Remove from refrigerator and allow to warm up for 2 hours.

Divide into two. Roll out on parchment to make two 10" circles. Cover with damp towel and rise double.

Make indentations with floured fingers and shake on kosher salt and grated cheese. Add onion rings, then drizzle on some olive oil.

Bake on tiles, 10-20 minutes @ 400 °F.
