Inner Beauty

Ingredients

  • 12 dried or fresh habanero chilis
  • 1 mango or bosc pear, peeled and pitted
  • 1 jar prepared yellow mustard
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup white vinegar
  • 1 Tbs curry powder
  • 1 Tbs cumin
  • 1 Tbs chili powder
  • ½ tsp black pepper, ground

Method

Warning: wear gloves.

Purée all in blender. Heat to boiling (with stirring) to sterilize, then jar and freeze.
