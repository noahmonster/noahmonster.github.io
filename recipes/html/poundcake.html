Cream Cheese Pound Cake

Ingredients

    A
  • 3 sticks butter at room temperature
  • 8-oz cream cheese at room temperature
    B
  • 3 cups sugar
    C
  • 6 eggs
    D
  • 3 cups flour
    E
  • 1 ½ tsp vanilla

Method

Beat A. Beat in B. Add C one at a time while beating. Add D slowly while beating. Add E.

Grease a 10-inch tube pan and line with waxed paper. Pour in batter and bake 1 ½ hours @ 325°
