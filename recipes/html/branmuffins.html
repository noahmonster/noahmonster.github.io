Bran Muffins

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1¼ cups bran
  • 4 Tbs brown sugar
  • 1 Tbs baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
    B
  • 1 fork-beaten egg
  • 1¼ cup milk
  • ¼ cup oil

Method

Sift A, then mix together. Combine B, then stir into A. Spoon into greased (or lined with paper or foil cups) 12-place muffin tin. Bake 20 minutes @ 400°
