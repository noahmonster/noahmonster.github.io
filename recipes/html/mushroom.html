Mushroom Soup

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 Tbs olive oil
  • 1 Tbs butter
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 1 diced celery
    B
  • 8 oz mushrooms, half chopped, half sliced
    C
  • 4 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 cup cooked white beans
  • 1 tsp salt
  • black pepper, ground
  • 2 Tbs flour
    D
  • ½ cup half and half

Method

Sir-fry A until golden, then add B and continue to stir-fry. Suspend the flour from C in some of the stock, then add all other C to pot and simmer. Stir in flour mixture and simmer 15 min. Remove from heat, cool slightly, then add tempered D.
