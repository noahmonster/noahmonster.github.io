Judy's Chicken or Turkey Curry

Ingredients

    A
  • 3 onions, sliced
    B
  • 1 slice ginger
  • 1 chopped green pepper
  • 1 chopped carrot
    C
  • 1-2 lbs chicken or turkey breast, sliced, or one cut-up chicken
    D
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 Tbs curry powder
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ tsp cayenne
  • ¼ tsp ground thyme
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¾ cup chicken broth or water
    E
  • almonds or mushrooms or peas

Method

Stir-fry A. Reserve. Stir-fry B. Add C and stir-fry. Add D and simmer 45 minutes. Add A and E. Simmer 15 minutes. Thicken with 2 Tbs cornstarch if desired.
