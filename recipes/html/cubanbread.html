Cuban Bread

Ingredients

  • 4-5 cups unbleached or bread flour
  • 1 pkg (2 tsp) yeast
  • 1 cup stone-ground whole wheat flour
  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups warm water

Method

Mix together to make a soft dough. Knead, rise until doubled. For a tangier loaf, rise longer -- about 2 hours.

Form into two baguettes. Place in baguette pan, cover with moist towel, and rise another ½ hour.

Place in cold oven and turn on @ 400°. Bake about 30-35 min.
