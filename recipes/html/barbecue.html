Barbecue Sauce

Ingredients

  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup vinegar
  • 6 Tbs (3/8 cup) oil
  • 6-oz can tomato paste
  • 6 Tbs (3/8 cup) Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 Tbs powdered mustard
  • 1 tsp black pepper, ground
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 2 Tbs brown sugar

Method

Combine and simmer 20 minutes.

For barbecued ribs or chicken: simmer meat 30 minutes so it is cooked. Pour off water from pot and add barbecue sauce. Marinate a few hours before barbecuing.
