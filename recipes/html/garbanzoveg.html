Garbanzo and Vegetable Curry

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 cups garbanzos (chickpeas) cooked
    B
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
    C
  • 1 ½ tsp paprika
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp cayenne
  • ½ tsp cumin, ground
  • ½ tsp black pepper, ground
  • ½ tsp powdered ginger
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 2 Tbs parsley
    D
  • 1 medium zucchini, cubed, not peeled
  • 28-oz can diced tomatoes
  • 2 Tbs raisins
    E
  • 1 cup frozen peas

Method

Prepare A and reserve. Stir-fry B. Add C briefly, then D and simmer 15 minutes. Add E.

Serve on couscous.
