Orange Bread

Ingredients

    A
  • grated zest (outer layer) of two oranges
  • ½ cup orange juice (squeeze that orange)
  • 1¼ cups warm water
  • 2 eggs
    B
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp (1 package) yeast
  • 6-7 cups flour

Method

Combine A. Combine B in a large bowl. Stir in A to form dough. Knead. Rise once until doubled. Form two loaves and put into greased pans. Rise until doubled. Bake ~35 minutes @ 375°.

For challah, Bake ~16-18 minutes @ 400°
