Chunky Vegetable Soup

Ingredients

    A
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 2 Tbs olive oil
  • 4 scallions, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
    B
  • 1 large carrot, sliced
  • 1 6-oz. zucchini, sliced
  • 1 small turnip, sliced, then halved
  • 4 oz green cabbage, shredded
  • 1 large potato, sliced, then halved
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 7 cups water
  • 1 can cannellini or navy beans, drained and rinsed
    C
  • 2 Tbs lemon juice
  • ground black pepper

Method

Stir-fry A. Add B and simmer covered 20 minutes. Add C.
