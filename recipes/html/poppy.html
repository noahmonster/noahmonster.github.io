Poppy Chicken

Ingredients

    A
  • 4 sliced onions
    B
  • 1 cut-up chicken
  • 1 sliced carrot
    C
  • 1 cup stock or water
  • ½ cup raisins
  • 1 dried chili pepper, crushed
  • 1 tsp poppy seeds
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper

Method

Stir-fry A. Reserve. Stir-fry B until browned. Add C and simmer covered 45 minutes. Add A. Thicken with 2 Tbs cornstarch if desired.

Alternative: After 25 minutes of cooking, add dumplings and simmer covered another 20 minutes.
