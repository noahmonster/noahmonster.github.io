Kugel

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup oil
  • ½ cup sugar
    B
  • 8 oz cottage cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • ½ tsp vanilla
  • ½ cup raisins
    C
  • 8 oz cooked noodles
    D
  • cinnamon

Method

Beat A. Add B. Add C. Put in greased 8" x 8" casserole. Sprinkle with D, then bake 45 min @ 350°.
