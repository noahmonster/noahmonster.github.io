Red Lentil Soup

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 cups red lentils
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 7 cups water or broth
    B
  • 1 large onion, diced
    C
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp cumin, grind
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds, grind
  • ½ tsp coriander, grind
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • pepper
    D
  • juice of 1 lemon

Method

Cook A about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally to keep lentils from clumping. Towards the end of this period stir-fry B in a little olive oil until browned. Add to lentils. Add C, cook another 5 minutes, discard the bay leaf, and add D.
