Irish Soda Bread

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 cups white flour
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 Tbs caraway seed
    B
  • 1 cup raisins
    C
  • 1 ¾ cup buttermilk or 1 ½ cup milk + 1 Tbs vinegar

Method

Sift A together, then stir to mix. Mix in B. Stir in C and form two loaves, handling as little as possible. Bake on tiles or on baking sheets, ~ 30 minutes @ 350°.
