Podvarak (Sauerkraut-Stuffed Turkey)

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 large onions, chopped
    B
  • ¼ cup parsley
  • 2 chopped garlic cloves (optional)
  • 3 dried red peppers, crushed
  • 1 Tbs paprika
  • 3 quart packages sauerkraut, drained
  • pepper
    C
  • 12-lb turkey

Method

Remove the giblets and neck from the turkey and reserve for baking giblet stock, below.

Stir-fry A. Add B (sauerkraut by handfuls) and continue to stir-fry until thoroughly hot and mixed. Stuff turkey and cover exposed stuffing with foil. Truss turkey's drumsticks and wings with string. Roast 30 minutes @ 500°. Then cover breast with a triangle of foil and roast @ 350° until breast reaches 161° and stuffing is at least 140°. Let stand at least 15 minutes before carving.

While turkey is cooking, make a stock from the giblets. Put them in a saucepan with about 1 cup of water and a halved onion, and simmer covered for about 2 hours. Pour off the stock and reserve.

After removing turkey from the pan, pour the drippings into a jar and cool. Later, discard any fat on top.

Make a gravy by combining the pan drippings and giblet stock and following the instructions for gravy.
