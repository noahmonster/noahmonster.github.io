Corn Muffins

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 4 Tbs brown sugar
  • 1 Tbs baking powder
    B
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 fork-beaten egg
  • 3 Tbs oil

Method

Sift A, then stir to mix. Mix B together. Add to A and mix minimally. Spoon into greased (or lined with paper or foil liners) muffin pan (12 muffins). Bake 12-15 minutes @ 425°.

Variation: Bake in loaf pan with foil or parchment lining, 35-40 minutes @ 400°.
