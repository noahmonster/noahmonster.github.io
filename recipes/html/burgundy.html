Beans Bourguinone (Burgundy Beans)

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 cups dried pintos, cooked dry
    B
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
    C
  • 1 cubed potato
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ 6-oz can tomato paste
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • 1 bay leaf    Warning: do not eat
    D
  • 1 cup burgundy
  • 1 tsp salt
    E
  • ½ lb mushrooms, sliced

Method

Prepare A. Stir-fry B until onions are translucent. Add C and simmer 20 minutes. Add A, D, and E and simmer 10 minutes.
