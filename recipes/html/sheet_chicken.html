Sheet-Pan Chicken & Veg's

Ingredients

    A
  • 3-4 chicken breasts or more drumettes
    B
  • Coarse-chopped veg's (broccoli, cauliflower, carrots, rutabaga, potato, winter squash)

Method

Line pan with parchment. Rub spice on A and place in pan. Toss B with olive oil and spices, then add. Bake ~35 min @ 400 °F. Brown under broiler if desired.
