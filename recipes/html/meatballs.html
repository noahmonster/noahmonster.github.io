Meatballs

Ingredients

    A
  • 3 lbs ground meat (best = 1 lb each beef round, lamb, pork)
  • 4 tsp worcestershire sauce
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • pepper

Method

Combine all. Form loose balls about golf-ball size or slightly larger. Bake on foil-lined pan ~ 18 minutes until internal temperature of 160 °F
