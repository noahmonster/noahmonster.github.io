Chumentashen

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp grated lemon rind
  • prune butter

Method

Beat butter and sugar together. Beat in eggs. Sift dry ingredients. Beat all but prune butter into sugar. Chill if necessary to firm dough, then roll out. Cut into 2" rounds (use tuna tin or large glass). Place a dab of prune butter in center of each and pinch up to partially seal. Bake 15 min @ 375°.
