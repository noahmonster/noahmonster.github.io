Oven-Baked Chuck Roast

Ingredients

    A
  • 3-4 lb chuck roast
    B
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 Tbs onion flakes
  • 2 bay leaves
    C
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
  • ¾ cup dry red wine
    C
  • 2 large carrots, split
  • Bunch of small potatoes, halved if large

Method

Preheat oven to 325°.

Tie the meat. Rub with salt and pepper. Sear the roast in a Dutch Oven pot, 3-4 minutes per side in olive oil.

Remove the meat and fry the onions and bay leaves until starting to brown, 7-10 minutes. Add onion flakes for another minute. Add C and bring to a boil.

Nestle meat back into pot, spooning sauce on top. Cover with parchment and lid. Bake 2 hours.

Tuck the potatoes and carrots into the sauce and bake another 2 hours.

Remove meat. Cook down sauce on stove if it needs to be thickened.
