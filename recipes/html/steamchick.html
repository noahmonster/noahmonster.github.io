Steamed Chicken and Chinese Sausages

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 cut up chicken
  • 2 Tbs cornstarch
  • 2 Tbs dark soy sauce
  • 2 Tbs white wine
  • 2 Tbs oil
    B
  • 4 chinese sausages sliced into ¼" pieces

Method

Mix A in in mixing bowl until coated. Put B on top. Place uncovered bowl in steamer (a large colander placed inside a stockpot containing about an inch of waer will work &mdash make sure the bottom of the colander is above the level of the water. Steam covered 1 ½ hours.
