Manhattan Clam Chowder

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 ½ Tbs diced salt pork or bacon (optional)
    B
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/3 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
    C
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 medium potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • black pepper, ground
  • 1 bay leaf (warning: do not eat)
  • ¼ tsp ground thyme
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes
    D
  • 2 6.5-oz cans chopped or baby clams, plus juice
  • 1 cup frozen corn

Method

Sir-fry A briefly, then add B and continue to stir-fry. Add C and simmer 20 minutes, covered. Add D and cook 5 minutes.
