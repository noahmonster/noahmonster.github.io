Clam Sauce for Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 10-oz canned baby clams
  • 1 smashed garlic clove
  • 1 Tbs dried parsley
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Method

Pour clam juice into saucepan with remaining ingredients, but reserve clams. Cook down (uncovered) to ½ of original volume. Remove from heat and add clams.
