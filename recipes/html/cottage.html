Cottage Pudding Cake

Ingredients

    A
  • ⅓ cup butter
  • ⅔ cup sugar
    B
  • 1 egg, beaten
    C
  • 2 ¼ cups flour
  • 5 tsp baking powder
    D
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Method

Cream A. Beat in B. Add C/D alternating. Spoon batter into greased 8" cake pan or flat casserole dish. Bake ~ 45 minutes @ 350°. Done when a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.
