Chicken or Veal Parmigiana

Ingredients

  • chicken breast filets or veal cutlets
  • bread crumbs
  • eggs
  • red spaghetti sauce
  • mozzarella cheese, sliced

Method

Dip meat in fork-beaten eggs, then dredge through a plate of bread crumbs. Fry in ¼" of oil, on medium-high, turning once, until golden on both sides. Drain on toweling.

Put some sauce in a casserole dish, put in the meat, and cover each with sauce, mozzarella, and more sauce. Bake uncovered 15-20 minutes until cheese is bubbly and starting to brown.
