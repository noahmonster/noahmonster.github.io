Carrot Cake

Ingredients

    A
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1½ cups oil
    B
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
    C
  • 1 cup golden raisins
  • 7 oz. shredded coconut
  • 8 oz. can crushed pineapple (drained)
  • 2½ cups grated carrots, squeezed dry
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
    D (topping)
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3 oz. cream cheese
  • 4 Tbs butter (½ stick)
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • ½ cup pecans (optional)

Method

Beat A. Sift B and add to A. Add C. Bake in buttered pan(s) @ 350° for ~ 1 hour.
