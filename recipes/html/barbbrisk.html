Oven Barbecued Brisket

Ingredients

  • 4 lb brisket
  • 2 tbs chili powder
  • 1 tbs salt
  • 1 tbs onion powder
  • 1 tbs ground black pepper
  • 1 tbs brown sugar
  • 2 tsp mustard powder
  • 1 can diced tomatoes

Method

Rub the meat with the spices and let sit about an hour. Roast on a rack for 60 min @ 350°. Move to covered pan, add tomatoes, and bake @ 300° for 3 hours.
