Honey Cake

Ingredients

    A
  • ½ cup oil
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
    B
  • 2 cups flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground ginger
    C
  • ½ cup honey
  • ½ cup cooled strong coffee
    D
  • ½ tsp vanilla
  • ¾ cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • grated orange rind

Method

Beat A until light. Sift B. Beat into to A in three batches, alternating with C. Add D. Bake in greased loaf pan @300° about 50 minutes. Dust with confectioner's sugar.
