Blueberry Muffins

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 cups white flour
  • ½ cup whole wheat flour
  • 5 Tbs sugar
  • 1 Tbs baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
    B
  • 1 fork-beaten egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ cup oil
    C
  • 1 cup blueberries

Method

Sift A, then mix together. Combine B, then stir into A briefly. Fold in C. Spoon into greased (or lined with paper or foil cups) 12-place muffin tin. Bake 20-22 minutes @ 400°
