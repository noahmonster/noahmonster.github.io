Oatmeal Bread

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1 ½ cups rolled oats (not quick cooking)
    B
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 Tbs brown sugar
  • ¼ cup oil
  • 1 Tbs salt
    C
  • 2 tsp (1 pkg) yeast
    D
  • ~ 6 cups flour

Method

Combine A and set aside to cool until lukewarm.

Combine B and warm in microwave 1 minute.

Mix A, B, and C. Add about 4 cups of D and mix well. Knead in enough D to form a soft dough that doesn't stick to the bowl. Cover with damp towel and rise till doubled, about 1 - 1 ½ hours.

Squash down dough, divide in two, and fold into loaves. Rise in lightly oiled pans covered with damp cloth, till doubled.

Bake ~ 30 minutes @ 350°. Done when nicely browned. Pop out of pan and rap on bottom — if not hollow sounding, put loaf directly on rack and bake another 5 min, or more.
