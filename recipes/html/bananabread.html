Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour (best if ½ whole wheat)
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup oil
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 Tbs vinegar or lemon juice
  • 2 mashed ripe bananas

Method

Sift the dry ingredients into a mixing bowl. Beat the eggs lightly, stir the oil and other liquids into them. Stir into the dry ingredients. Stir in bananas. Bake in greased loaf pan @ 350° about 1 hour.
