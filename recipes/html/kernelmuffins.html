Corn Kernel Muffins

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • 1 Tbs baking powder
  • 2 Tbs sugar
    B
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 fork-beaten egg
  • 3 Tbs oil
    C
  • 1 cup frozen or canned corn

Method

Sift A and mix together. Mix B. Add B and C to A and mix minimally. Spoon into greased (or lined with paper or foil liners) 12-place muffin tin. Bake 15-18 minutes @ 425°
