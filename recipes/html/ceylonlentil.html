Ceylon Lentil Curry

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 cup dried lentils, preferably red
    B
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 garlic cloves, smashed
  • black pepper, ground
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
    C
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
    D
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 1 garlic clove, smashed
  • ½ tsp black mustard seed
    E
  • ½ cup milk

Method

Soak A in water at least an hour. Drain, just cover with fresh water, add B, and simmer uncovered 15-30 minutes until tender and rather dry. Add C.

Stir-fry onions of D until golden, then add garlic and mustard seed briefly. Add to lentils. Add E and cook 5 minutes.
