Fat Free Indian Cornbread

Ingredients

    A
  • 4 egg whites
    B
  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
    C
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 cup canned pumpkin
  • ½ cup dried fruit (e.g., raisins)
  • ½ cup walnuts (optional)

Method

Beat A to stiff-peak stage. Sift B into a bowl and stir to mix. Mix C together, then stir into B. Fold in A. Line the bottom of a nonstick loaf pan with parchment or foil, add batter, bake 80 minutes @ 300°
