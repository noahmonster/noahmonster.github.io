# Potato Latkes
## Ingredients

### A
* 6 medium potatoes
* 1½ onions 

### B
* 2 eggs
* 2 Tbs matzo meal
* 1 tsp salt 

## Method

Grate A, then let stand for a few minutes and squeeze out excess liquid.
Mix in B. Form into patties and fry in about ¾ inch oil on medium high heat.
Turn to brown both sides. Drain on paper towels.
