Scallop Bisque

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 ¾ cups milk
  • 1 clove, ground
  • 1 bay leaf (warning: do not eat)
  • black pepper, ground
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 Tbs chopped onion
  • 3 Tbs butter (optional)
    B
  • ¼ cup milk
    C
  • 2 Tbs flour
    D
  • 1 lb. small scallops

Method

Simmer A 15 minutes. Slowly add B to C while stirring, to produce a lump-free paste. Stir into A. Simmer another 15 minutes. Add D and simmer 10 minutes.
