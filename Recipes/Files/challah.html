Challah

Ingredients

  • 4-5 cups unbleached or bread flour
  • 1 pkg (2 tsp) yeast
  • 3 eggs
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup oil
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water

Optional Glaze

  • ½ tsp cornstarch in ½ cup water, microwave to thicken

Method

Mix together. Knead, rise until doubled.

Divide dough into three (standard) or four (top challah) even amounts. Roll into snakes about 2 feet long. Widely braid the three snakes on a sheet of parchment. For top challah, divide the remaining snake into three and roll out each one. Braid these into a long, narrow challah and glue it on top of the main challah with water or the glaze. Cover with a moist towel and rise again.

Brush the challah gently with the glaze, then bake @ 375°s about 20 min. Brush again with glaze after removing from the oven.
