Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • ¾ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg
  • ¾ cup oil
  • 2 Tbs peanut butter
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • raisins and/or chocolate chips

Method

Sift together the flour and baking soda. Add cinnamon and nutmeg. Slowly stir the oil into the peanut butter, then add it and the sugar, eggs, and vanilla to the flour and beat until smooth. Stir in the oats and the raisins and/or chips.

Drop onto greased sheets and bake about 14 minutes @ 350°.
