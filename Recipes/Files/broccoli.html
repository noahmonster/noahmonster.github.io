Szechuan Broccoli

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 bunch broccoli
    B
  • 2 dried red peppers, seeded and crushed
  • ½ piece of ginger root, peeled and cut like matchsticks
    C
  • 1 tsp salt
    D
  • 2/3 cup water
    E
  • 1 Tbs cornstarch dispersed in ¼ cup water

Method

Separate stalks of A. Remove tough bottoms and discard. Cut off florets into chunks. Cut larger stems in half lengthwise, then into quarters, then into 2" pieces.

Heat wok on high. Add oil. When small bubbles and wisps of smoke appear, stir-fry B 5 seconds. Add A and stir-fry 1 ½ minutes. Add C and stir-fry 1 minute. Add D, cover, and steam 8 minutes, reducing heat if necessary.

Stir in D.
