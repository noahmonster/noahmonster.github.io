Mary's Cheesecake

Ingredients

    A
  • 3 8-oz cream cheese at room temperature
    B
  • 5 eggs
    C
  • 1 cup sugar
    D
  • 1 tsp vanilla
    E
  • 8-oz sour cream
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
    F
  • 1 can cherry topping or pie filling

Method

Beat A till creamy. Add B one at a time while beating. Add C while beating, then D. Bake in glass casserole dish ~ 40 minutes @ 325° till firm. Cool 20 minutes (center will fall).

Mix E. Spread on top of cake and bake another 10 minutes. Allow to cool. Top with F.
