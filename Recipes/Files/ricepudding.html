Creamy Rice Pudding

Ingredients

    A
  • 5 Tbs long-grain rice
  • ½ cup water
    B
  • 2 cups milk
    C
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ Tbs sugar
  • raisins (optional)

Method

Soak A 2 hours. Add B, and either 1)heat to boiling in microwave, about 7 minutes on high, watching carefully for boilover, or 2) heat to near boiling on stovetop, medium-high heat, stirring constantly to avoid burning.

Lower heat as low as possible and simmer covered about 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes. Remove from heat when creamy, being aware that pudding will thicken a little as it cools.

Add C.
