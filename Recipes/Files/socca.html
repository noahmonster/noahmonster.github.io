Socca

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 cup garbanzo flour
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 ½ Tbs olive oil

Method

Mix A until smooth, then allow to sit for an hour.

Meanwhile, move an oven rack up to the broiling position and preheat the oven to 450°

When ready to cook, put a 10" cast-iron skillet in the oven for 5 minutes. Remove it, and turn the broiler on. Oil the skillet lightly, and pour the batter in. Level if needed.

Place socca under the broiler for 5-8 minutes, until the top is lightly browned and the edges are crispy.

Remove the socca to a cutting board and top with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
