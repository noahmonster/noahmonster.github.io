Moorghi Khari

Ingredients

    A
  • 4 onions, sliced
    B
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
    C
  • 2 cloves, ground
  • seeds from 4 cardamom pods, ground
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½-1 tsp cayenne
    D
  • 1 jointed chicken, or equivalent amount of parts
    E
  • 2 cups broth or water
    F
  • 3 Tbs powdered milk dissolved in 2/3 cup water (optional)

Method

Stir-fry A until lightly browned. Add B and stir-fry briefly. Add C and stir-fry briefly. Add D and stir-fry. Add E, bring to a boil, then simmer covered about 1 hour until tender. Add F. Thicken if desired with cornstarch (1-2 Tbs dissolved in ¼ cup cold water).
