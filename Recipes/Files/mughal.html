Mughal Chicken

Ingredients

    A
  • 8 chicken thighs
    B
  • seeds from 5 cardamon pods, ground
  • 3 bay leaves (warning: do not eat)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp cumin, ground
  • 1 tsp corriander, ground
  • ¼ tsp cayenne
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 cloves, ground
  • 3 Tbs raisins

Method

Skin A by gripping an edge of skin with a paper towel and pulling off.

Stir-fry A. Add B and simmer 50 minutes. Remove chicken and cook down sauce to thicken.
