Doro Wat (Ethiopian Chicken)

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 jointed chicken, or 8 thighs
    B
  • ¼ cup lemon juice + 2 cups water
    C
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 ½ tsp paprika
  • ¼ tsp cayenne
  • ½ tsp ground ginger
    D
  • 1 Tbs flour mixed in 2 Tbs water and 2 Tbs lemon juice

Method

Soak A in B 15 minutes. Drain. Cook in C 45 minutes. Remove chicken and thicken with D. Return chicken to pot.
