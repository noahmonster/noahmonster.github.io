Indian Spinach

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 onion, chopped
    B
  • 1" ginger root, grated
    C
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • 2 cloves, ground
  • ½ tsp cardamom seed, ground
  • ½ tsp coriander, ground
  • ½ tsp cumin, ground
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp dried ground habanero
    D
  • 2 pkg fresh spinach, chopped
    E
  • ½ can diced tomatoes
    F
  • sliced mushrooms (optional)
    G
  • 1 Tbs sour cream

Method

Fry A. Add B towards the end. Add C. Add D, E, F and simmer gently 30 min. Put G in a bowl, add a few spoonfuls of the hot spinach and stir in to temper (prevents curdling). Stir this mixture into the main spinach.
