Mexican Salsa

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 onions, chopped
    B
  • 28-oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp cumin, ground
  • ¼ tsp coriander
  • ¼ tsp cayenne
  • ¼ tsp pepper, ground
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • ½ 6-oz can tomato paste
  • 2 Tbs red wine

Method

Stir-fry A till translucent. Add B and simmer 30 minutes.
