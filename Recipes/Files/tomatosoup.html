Tomato Soup

Ingredients

    A
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
    B
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 bay leaf Warning: Do not eat
    C
  • 15 oz. can crushed tomatoes + 1 can water
  • ground black pepper
  • salt to taste

Method

Stir-fry A till onions are translucent. Add B and stir-fry briefly. Add C and simmer 15 minutes.
