Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 1 cup long-grain rice

Method

In a heavy saucepan with a tight lid, bring the water to a boil. Add the rice, cover, and turn down heat to the lowest possible. After 15 minutes turn off the heat. Wait another 5 minutes before opening.

Variations: This is a very firm rice. For softer rice use 1 ¾ cups water. For flavored rice use 2 cups stock.
