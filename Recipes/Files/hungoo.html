Hungarian Beef Goulash

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 onions, chopped
    B
  • 2 lbs beef, cut into chunks
    C
  • 1 pack sauerkraut
  • 1 Tbs paprika
  • 6-oz can tomato paste
  • 1 tsp caraway
    D
  • 8 oz sour cream

Method

Stir-fry A and reserve. Stir-fry B until browned. Add C and cook 45 minutes covered. Add A and cook 15 minutes. Remove from heat and add D. Serve on noodles.
