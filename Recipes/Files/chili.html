Chili

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 lb (2 cups) dried black or other beans, cooked
    B
  • 2 large onions, chopped
    C
  • 2 smashed garlic cloves
    D
  • 2 lb ground round
    E
  • 3 Tbs chili powder
  • 2 Tbs vinegar
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • ½ tsp coriander, ground
  • 2 tsp cumin, ground
  • 2 15-oz cans diced tomatoes

Method

Stir-fry B. Add C briefly, then add D and stir-fry until chunks are all separate and no longer red. Add A and E and simmer covered (uncovered if too thin) for 45 minutes.

Quick Version: Use 3 cans of cooked beans.
