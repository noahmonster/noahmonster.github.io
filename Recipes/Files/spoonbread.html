Thanksgiving Sweet Potato Spoonbread

Ingredients

    A
  • 3 medium sweet potatoes
  • 3 Tbs butter
  • 2 tsp baking powder
    B
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 Tbs maple syrup
  • ½ salt
  • 4 Tbs cornmeal suspended in ½ cup milk
    C
  • 4 eggs, separated

Method

A: Bake potatoes until soft, about 1-1½ hours. Mash in mixer with butter and baking powder, then allow to cool.

B: While potatoes are cooking, heat milk, syrup, and salt in heavy saucepan. When hot, stir in cornmeal slurry. Stir several minutes until thickened, then cool.

C: When everything is cool: Mix egg yolks and cornmeal into potatoes. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks, then fold into the batter. Bake in a buttered glass dish 25-35 min.
