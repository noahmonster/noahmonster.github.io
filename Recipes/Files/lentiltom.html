Lentil and Tomato Spaghetti Sauce

Ingredients

    A
  • ½ cup lentils
    B
  • 1 onion, chopped
    C
  • 1 ½ tsp dried basil
  • 1 ½ tsp dried oregano
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 bay leaf   Warning: do not eat

Method

Soak A for at least 1 hour. Drain excess water until just covered, then simmer 15-30 minutes in a covered pot until done.

Stir-fry B. Add C and simmer 20 minutes. Add A.
