Fat Free Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour (best if ½ whole wheat)
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 3 egg whites
  • 5/8 cup milk
  • 1 Tbs vinegar or lemon juice
  • 2 mashed ripe bananas

Method

Beat the egg whites until they form stiff peaks. Sift the dry ingredients into a mixing bowl. Mix the liquids together, then stir into the dry ingredients. Stir in bananas. Fold in the egg whites. Bake in greased loaf pan @ 300° about 65 minutes.
