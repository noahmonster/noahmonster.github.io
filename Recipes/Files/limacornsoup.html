Lima Corn Chowder

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 pkg baby lima beans, cooked in minimal water.
    B
  • 1 onion, chopped,
  • 3 celery stalks, chopped
    C
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1 tsp basil
  • ½ tsp thyme
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2 Tbs soy sauce
  • 1 cup milk

Method

Prepare A. Stir-fry B, or microwave 2 minutes. Add B and C to beans. Simmer 10 minutes.
