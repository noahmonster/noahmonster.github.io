Tomato Chutney

Ingredients

    A
  • 3 dried red chilis
  • ½ tsp cumin, ground
  • 3 cloves garlic, smashed
  • ½ tsp salt
    B
  • 10 large peeled tomatoes, smashed
    C
  • 1 tsp butter
  • 3 onions, chopped
    D
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Method

Note: Peel tomatoes by immersing in boiling water for 1 minute. Remove, cool, and peel.

Pulverize A. Simmer with B until reduced to a thick paste. Add C for 5 min, then remove from heat and add D.
