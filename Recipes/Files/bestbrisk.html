Braised Brisket

Ingredients

  • 4-5 lb brisket
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 sliced onions
  • 2 cups red wine
  • 4 coarsely chopped carrots
  • 1 15-oz. can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cups water

Method

Sear the meat on both sides. Place the onions on the bottom of a dutch oven and place the meat on them. Add everything else. Bake ~ 5.5 hours @ 325°. Chill overnight. Remove meat and slice. Cook down liquids if necessary, then add meat to heat and/or cook a little more if tough.
