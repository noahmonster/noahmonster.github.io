Mayonaise

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 egg yolk
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp mustard powder
    B
  • 2 tsp lemon juice + 1 Tbs vinegar
    C
  • 1 cup canola oil

Method

Whisk A. Add ½ of B. Very slowly whisk in at a drizzle ½ of B. Whisk in remainder of B, then slowly whisk in remainder of C. Let stand 1 hour before refrigerating. (Source: Alton Brown)
