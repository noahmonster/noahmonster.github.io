Scottish Oatmeal Bread

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1 ½ cups Scottish (Irish) steel-cut oats
The following is for each of two batches:
    B
  • ½ A
  • 3/8c powdered milk
  • 3 Tbs brown sugar
  • 1/2 grated ginger chunk (walnut sized)
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 ½ tsp yeast
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • 3-4 cups flour
    D
  • 1 cup warm water

Method

Combine A and set aside to cool until lukewarm.

Do this in two batches, then combine the batches: Combine B. Add D while mixing. Knead to form a soft dough, adding the 4th cup of flour as needed to prevent sticking to the bowl. Cover with damp towel and rise till doubled, about 1 - 1 ½ hours.

Squash down dough, divide in three, and fold into loaves. Rise in lightly oiled pans covered with damp cloth, till doubled.

Bake ~ 20 minutes @ 400°. Done when nicely browned. Pop out of pan and rap on bottom — if not hollow sounding, put loaf directly on rack and bake another 5 min, or more.
