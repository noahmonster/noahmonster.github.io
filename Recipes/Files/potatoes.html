Indian Potatoes

Ingredients

    A
  • 8 medium red or gold potatoes
    B
  • 1 onion, sliced
    C
  • ¼ tsp anise seed, ground
  • ¼ tsp mustard seed, ground
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 bay leaf (REMOVE BEFORE SERVING!)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp dried ground habanero
    D
  • 1 can diced tomatoes

Method

Boil A until just tender (~ 30-40 minutes). Peel, quarter, and set aside to dry. Fry B. Add C and stir briefly, then add A and stir to coat. Add D. Simmer gently 30 minutes.
