Spaghetti Sauce Doopiaza

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 clove garlic, smashed
    B
  • 4 onions, chopped
    C
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp cayenne
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • seeds of 4 cardamom pods, ground
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½" piece of ginger, peeled and minced
    D
  • 28-oz can diced tomatoes

Method

Stir-fry A until golden, then discard. Stir-fry B. Add C and fry briefly. Add D and simmer 20 minutes.
