Stuffed Cabbage

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 head green cabbage
    B (Slow cookers – pick and choose)
  • onion
  • celery
  • fennel
  • carrot
    C (Medium cookers – pick and choose)
  • green pepper
  • asparagus
  • tomatillo
  • okra
  • potato
  • sweet potato
  • green beans
    D (Fast cookers – pick and choose)
  • mushrooms
  • olives
  • cooked grain (rice, bulgur, kasha)
  • cooked garbanzo beans
    E
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes

Method

Note: This is an infinitely variable recipe. Use whatever ingredients you'd like. The filling can be stir-fried or microwaved. The completed recipe can be heated in the microwave or oven.

Cut a cone-shaped hole in the bottom of the cabbage to free the leaves. Put the cabbage in a large pot of boiling water to parboil and release the leaves. As the leaves soften, release them one-by-one by slipping a large spoon (a slotted spoon works well) under them and prying off. Remove leaves to a plate and then to the refrigerator.

Prepare the filling by microwaving or stir-frying B 5-10 minutes until the carrots have softened but still have some bite. Add C for another 5-10 minutes. Add D and half of E and continue another 5 minutes until thoroughly heated (160°F if you have a thermometer). Add salt, pepper, soy sauce, etc. to taste. Chill.

Fill each leaf with a scoop of filling, fold in the ends and roll up. Tie with cotton kitchen string. Layer in a small casserole dish, then cover with remaining E. When ready to serve microwave 10 minutes.
