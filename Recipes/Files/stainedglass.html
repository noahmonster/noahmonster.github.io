Stained Glass

Ingredients

  • ½ stick butter
  • 12 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1 bag tiny colored marshmallows
  • shredded coconut

Method

Melt butter and chips in microwave, about 4 minutes, in large bowl. Stir in walnuts and marshmallows. Sprinkle coconut onto waxed paper, pour on the chocolate mixture and form into a log. Refrigerate several hours until firm. Slice with serrated knife.
