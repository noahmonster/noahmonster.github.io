Chullent

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 sliced onions
    B
  • 1 lb baby limas, soaked overnight
  • ½ cup pearled barley (optional)
    C
  • 2 lbs stew beef
    D
  • 2 Tbs flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • pepper
  • ½ tsp paprika
    E
  • Boiling water

Method

Stir-fry A. Add B. Add C and D and mix. Add F to cover. Simmer 3-4 hours.

Note: Works well in crock-pot with 8 hour cooking time.
