Zucchini/Squash Bread

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 cups white flour
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
    B
  • 2 eggs, fork beaten
  • ½ cup milk
  • 3 Tbs oil
  • 1 tsp vinegar
    C
  • 2 cups shredded summer squash
    D
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Method

Sift A into a mixing bowl. Mix B. Stir into A. Stir in C and D. Bake in greased pullman loaf pan (or two smaller pans) @ 350° about 1 hour.
