European 100% Rye

Sourdough Preparation

    Day1 p.m.
  • ½ cup warm water
  • ¼ cup rye flour
  • 1/8 tsp yeast
    Day2 p.m.
  • ¼ cup warm water
  • ¾ cup rye flour
    Day3 a.m.
  • ¼ cup warm water
  • ¾ cup rye flour
    Day3 p.m.
  • ¼ cup warm water
  • ½ cup rye flour

Baking Day Ingredients (Day4)

    A
  • Sourdough from above
    B
  • ~6 cups Rye flour
  • 1 Tbs salt
    C
  • 3 cups warm water

Method

Sourdough: Mix the Day1 ingredients together in a large bowl. Cover loosely with plastic wrap. Keep in a warm place to allow fermentation. Add the remaining ingredients on the days indicated.

Bread: Combine A, B, and C. This will be a very sticky dough (more like a thick batter). Rise for 5 hours (won't rise much). Put into two greased loaf pans. Rise about 2 hours. Bake @ 300° for 2½-3 hours. Done when dark brown on top and inserted toothpick comes out clean.
