Pizza

Dough (2 pizzas)

    A
  • ~ 6 cups flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp (1 pkg) yeast
    B
  • 2 cups warm water
  • ¼ cup olive oil (or other oil)

Sauce

    C
  • 1 chopped onion
    D
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed and chopped
    E
  • 28-oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • salt
  • pepper

Topping

    F
  • 1 lb mozzarella (or ½ each mozzarella and provolone)

Method

Dough: Combine A. Add B and knead. Rise twice in warmed oven (turn on heat for one minute to warm).

Sauce: Fry C. Add D briefly. Add E and simmer 30 min.

Pizza: Divide dough into two. Roll out onto foil or parchment. Brush with oil. Cover lightly with sauce (2 laddles per pizza). Add cheese and other toppings. Bake @ 500 °F 5-10 minutes.
