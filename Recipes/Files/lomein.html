Seafood LoMein

Ingredients

    A
  • ¾ lb scallops or shrimp or fish fillet
    B
  • ½ pkg whole wheat angel hair pasta
    C
  • 1 onion, chopped coarse
    D
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1-2 carrots, chopped
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • other vegetables, such as shredded cabbage and/or chopped asparagus
    E
  • 1 walnut-sized chunk ginger root, grated
    F
  • 1 Tbs tempura or soy sauce
  • 1 Tbs dark soy
  • 1 Tbs hoisin sauce
  • ground pepper
  • 1/8 tsp habanero flakes

Method

Boil A 5 min, set aside. Boil B, wash several times in cold water, set aside.

Stir-fry C, then D, trying to add the ones requiring the most cooking (celery, carrots) first, and the others later.

Add E, then add back A until hot, then B. When hot, add F. Cover on low heat for another 5 min.
