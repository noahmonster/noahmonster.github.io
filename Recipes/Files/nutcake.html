Passover Nut Cake

Ingredients

    A
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup matzo meal
  • grated orange rind
  • 1 cup ground nuts (use blender)
  • ½ cup orange juice
    B
  • 6 egg whites, beaten until stiff

Method

Mix A. Fold in B. Bake @ 325° for about 60 minutes in a greased loaf pan. Test for doneness with butter knife.
