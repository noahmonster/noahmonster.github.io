Stuffed Peppers and Tomatoes

Ingredients

    A
  • 4 peppers and 4 tomatoes
    B
  • 2 lbs ground round
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup cooked rice
  • ½ tsp salt
  • pepper
    C
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes

Method

cut 2" hole in tops of A. Discard pepper seeds. Scoop out tomato innards and add to C. Mix B and stuff into A. Arrange in pot and add C. Simmer covered 1 ½ hours.
