Trinidad Shrimp or Turkey Curry

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 onions, sliced
    B
  • 2 lbs sliced turkey breast or shrimp
    C
  • 1 Tbs fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 ½ tsp cumin, ground
  • 1 ½ tsp mustard seed, ground
  • 1 ½ tsp peppercorns, ground
  • 1 ½ tsp turmeric
  • ¼ tsp cayenne
  • 1 bay leaf (warning: do not eat)
    D
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 Tbs lemon juice

Method

Stir-fry A. Stir-fry B, if turkey — if shrimp, hold till end. Add C and stir-fry briefly. Add D and simmer 30 minutes. If using shrimp, add in last 5 minutes. If desired, thicken with cornstarch. Discard bay leaf before serving.
