Pizza Bread

Ingredients

    A
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 pkg (2 tsp) yeast
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup water
    B
  • garlic powder
  • pepper
    C
  • ½ can tomato paste
    D
  • dried oregano
  • 2 Tbs chopped onion, fried or microwaved 1 minute

Method

Mix A together to make a soft dough. Knead, rise once.

Roll out dough to a rectangle and place on a lightly greased baking sheet. Sprinkle with B. Spread with C, then top with D.

Bake ~ 10 minutes @ 400°
