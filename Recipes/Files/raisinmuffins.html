Raisin Muffins

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 ½ cup white flour
  • 4 Tbs brown sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¾ tsp cinnamon
  • 1 Tbs baking powder
    B
  • 1 ¼ cup milk
  • 1 fork-beaten egg
  • 3 Tbs oil

Method

Sift A and mix to combine. Combine B, then stir minimally into A. Spoon into greased (or paper or foil-lined) 12-place muffin tin. Bake 15-18 minutes @ 400°.
