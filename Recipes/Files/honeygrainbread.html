Honey Grain Bread

Ingredients (three loaves)

    A
  • 1½ cups white flour
  • 1½ cups wheatless 8-grain cereal (Bob's Red Mill)
  • 3/8 tsp yeast
  • 2 cups water
    B – need two batches of this
  • 1½ tsp salt
  • ¾ tsp yeast
  • ¾ cup water
  • 3 Tbs honey
  • 3½-4 cups white flour

Method

Prepare the poolish (sponge) the night before. Mix A at least 100 strokes. Cover and allow to ferment overnight.

Divide A into two. Add one batch of B to each and knead. Combine the two batches and rise until doubled. Divide into three loaves and rise again until doubled. Bake in pans or freeform 20-30 minutes @ 400 °F.
