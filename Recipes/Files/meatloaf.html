Meat Loaf

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped carrot
    B
  • 2 lbs ground round
  • 1 cup rolled oats or old-fashioned oats
  • pepper
  • 1 cup tomato juice or 3 Tbs ketchup + water
  • 1 fork-beaten egg
  • 1 Tbs loose tea (optional)
  • ¼ tsp celery seed
  • 1 Tbs dried parsley
  • 1 tsp salt

Method

Stir-fry A. Combine A and B. Bake in loaf pan @ 350° for 75 minutes. For easy removal line the bottom of the pan with aluminum foil or parchment.
