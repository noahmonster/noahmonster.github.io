Jewish Rye/Pumpernickel

Sourdough Preparation

    Day1 p.m.
  • 1 cup warm water
  • ½ cup rye flour
  • 1/8 tsp yeast
  • 1 Tbs caraway seeds
  • 1 Tbs chopped onion (or dried)
    Day2 p.m.
  • ½ cup warm water
  • 1½ cup rye flour
    Day3 a.m.
  • ½ cup warm water
  • 1 cup rye flour
    Day3 p.m.
  • ½ cup warm water
  • 1 cup rye flour

Baking Day Ingredients (Day4)

    A
  • Sourdough from above, divided into two batches
    B – need two batches of this
  • 4-5 cups white flour
  • 1 Tbs salt
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • Pumpernickel: 1 Tbs cocoa + 1 Tbs caramel color
    C – need two of this
  • 1 cup warm water

Method

Sourdough: Mix the Day1 ingredients together in a large bowl. Cover loosely with plastic wrap. Keep in a warm place to allow fermentation. Add the remaining ingredients on the days indicated.

Bread: Process in two batches. For each batch, knead A, B, and C. Combine the batches after kneading. Rise until doubled. Then roll out the dough into a log and divide into three loaves. Rise until doubled. Bake @ 375° for about 30 minutes .
