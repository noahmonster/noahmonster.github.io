Biscuits

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 Tbs baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
    B
  • 3 Tbs oil or ½ stick butter
    C
  • ¾ cup milk

    Method

    Sift A. Work in B with clean hands, grinding the clumps of oil between your fingers until uniformly distributed.

    Add C with a minimum of stirring, gather dough together, and pat out into a square shape on a floured counter. (The more you handle this dough the tougher the biscuits will be.)

    Cut dough in thirds both ways to form nine biscuits, transfer to a baking sheet, and bake @ 450° 10-12 minutes until golden.
