Potato Bread

Ingredients (two loaves)

    A
  • 2 medium gold or red potatoes, grated
  • ½ onion, grated
  • 2/3 cup powdered milk
  • 1 Tbs vinegar
  • 1 Tbs caraway seed
  • 1 Tbs salt
  • 1 pkg (2 tsp) yeast
  • 2 cups rye or whole wheat flour
  • ~ 4 cups white flour
  • 1 ¾ cup water

Ingredients (three loaves – process in two batches)

    A – need two batches of this
  • 2 small gold or red potatoes, grated
  • 1 small onion, grated
  • ½ cup powdered milk
  • 1 Tbs vinegar
  • 1 Tbs caraway seed
  • 1 Tbs salt
  • 2 tsp yeast
  • 1 ½ cups rye or whole wheat flour
  • 3-3 ½ cups white flour
  • 1 ¼ water

Method

Knead (for three loaves, knead in two batches, then combine). Cover with damp towel. Rise in a warm place until doubled. Divide into two, fold into loaves. Rise in lightly greased pans until doubled. Bake 30 min @ 375°. Done when nicely browned. Pop out of pan and rap on bottom — if not hollow sounding, put loaf directly on rack and bake another 5 min, or more.
