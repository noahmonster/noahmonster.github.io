Basic Vegetable Stock

Ingredients

    A
  • 4 onions, chopped
  • 2 Tbs olive oil
  • 2 large carrots, coarsely chopped
  • 2 large stalks celery with leaves, coarsely chopped
    B
  • 1/2 pound fresh mushrooms, coarsely chopped
    C
  • 3 large cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon dry basil
  • ⅔ cup white wine or beer
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 4 quarts water
  • 6 large romaine lettuce leaves, coarsely chopped

Method

Stir-fry A until onions are golden. Add B and fry. Add C and cook 90 min.
