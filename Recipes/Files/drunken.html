Drunken Beans

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 cups dried pintos, cooked
    B
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 jalapeños or dried red chilis, chopped
    C
  • 1 bottle dark beer
  • 15-oz can diced tomatoes
    D
  • salt
  • pepper

Method

Prepare A. Stir-fry B and add to A. Add C. Cook down if necessary, then season with D.
