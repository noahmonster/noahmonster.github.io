Matzo Stuffing

Ingredients

  • 4 matzos, soak in water then squeeze out excess
  • 2 onions, chopped and stir-fried
  • 4 eggs
  • ~ ¼ cup matzo meal

Method

Combine all and bake in greased casserole @ 325° for 30-45 minutes.

Alternative: stuff into chicken or turkey. The center of the stuffing must reach 160°.
