Shepherd's Pie

Ingredients

    A
  • 5 large potatoes
  • milk
  • paprika
    B
  • 1 ½ lbs ground round
  • ½ onion, chopped
    C
  • frozen mixed vegetables
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 Tbs ketchup
  • pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 Tbs flour

Method

Peel, then boil potatoes. Mash with milk and reserve.

Fry onion and meat. Remove from heat. Stir in C. Scoop into greased casserole.

Spoon mashed potatoes on top of meat. Make ridges with fork and sprinkle with paprika.

Bake 30 min @350°. If top is not crisp enough, put it under the broiler while watching carefully.
