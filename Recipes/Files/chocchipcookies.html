Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2¼ cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 sticks butter
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 12 oz. chocolate chips

Method

Combine flour and baking soda and reserve. Beat sugars and butter until pale, light, and fluffy. Beat in vanilla, then eggs. Add the flour mixture ½ cup at a time. Mix in chips.

Drop by tablespoons onto ungreased sheets and bake about 10-12 minutes @ 375°. Cool on rack.
