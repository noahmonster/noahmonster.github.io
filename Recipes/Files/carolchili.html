Carol's Chili

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 cup black or other beans, cooked, or two 15-oz cans
    B
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
    C
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 butternut or acorn squash, peeled and cubed
    D
  • 3 Tbs chili powder
  • 2 15-oz cans diced tomatoes
    E
  • salt
  • pepper

Method

Prepare A. Stir-fry B until onions are translucent, then add C and continue to stir-fry. Add D and simmer 15 minutes until potatoes are done. Add A. Season with E.
