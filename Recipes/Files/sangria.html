Sangria

Ingredients

    A
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ¾ cup water
    B
  • 1 orange, sliced thinly
  • 1 lime, sliced thinly
    C
  • 1 quart chilled red wine
  • 10-oz club soda

Method

Boil A until the sugar dissolves. Remove from heat. Add B, cover, and let soak 4 hours. Add C and chill or add ice cubes.
