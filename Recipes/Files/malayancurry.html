Malayan Curry

Ingredients

    A
  • 4 onions, sliced
  • 2 slices ginger
    B
  • 1-2 lbs cubed meat
    C
  • ¼ tsp cayenne
  • 2 Tbs curry powder
  • 2 Tbs flour
    D
  • 15-oz can chopped tomatoes
  • can from tomatoes, filled with milk
    E
  • 1 Tbs lemon juice

Method

Stir-fry A. Reserve. If using meat, stir-fry B. Add C and continue to stir-fry. Add D and simmer 35 minutes. Add back A for another 10 minutes. Add E.

Instructions for fish or shrimp: Omit flour. Add fish in last 10 minutes to avoid overcooking. For shrimp, add in last 5 minutes.
