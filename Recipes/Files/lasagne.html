Lasagne

Ingredients

A B
3 Tbs olive oil 6-oz can tomato paste
2 chopped onions 15-oz can diced tomatoes
4 cloves garlic, chopped 8-oz can tomato sauce
1 lb ground beef  
3 tsp oregano C
2 tsp parsley 1 lb box lasagne
pepper  
D
1 lb ricotta cheese
1 lb mozzarella cheese, sliced
½ cup grated parmesan or romano cheese

Method

Stir-fry the onions in the oil, add garlic, meat and spices and continue until meat is cooked. Add B and simmer 30-90 minutes.

Cook the lasagne noodles in boiling water until still firm but no longer hard. Drain and refill pot with cold water. Leave noodles in water until needed.

Layer the casserole as follows:

  • sauce (2 ladles)
  • noodles
  • ricotta (dabbed on)
  • mozzarella
  • sauce
  • parmesan
Repeat for additional layers (~3 total)

Final layer:

  • noodles
  • sauce
  • parmesan

Bake at 375° for 30-45 minutes, until bubbly and browning in sections.
