Brined Roast Turkey

Ingredients

    A
  • 1 cup kosher salt
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 Tbs peppercorns
  • 1 pkg Knorr vegetable soup, dry mix
  • ½ Tbs allspice berries
  • 1 cup water
    B
  • Sterilized (10% bleach) bucket
  • cold water
  • ice cubes
    C
  • 12 lb turkey

Method

Boil A, then cool.

Remove the giblets and neck from the turkey and reserve for making giblet stock, see below.

Place turkey in bucket and add brine, then add water and ice cubes to cover. Cover bucket with plate and soak at least 12 hours in a cold place.

Remove turkey. Truss turkey's drumsticks and wings with string. Roast 30 minutes @ 500°. Then cover breast with a triangle of foil and roast @ 350° until breast reaches 161° and stuffing is at least 140°. Let stand at least 15 minutes before carving.

While turkey is cooking, make a stock from the giblets. Put them in a saucepan with about 1 cup of water and a halved onion, and simmer covered for about 2 hours. Pour off the stock and reserve.

After removing turkey from the pan, pour the drippings into a jar and cool. Later, discard any fat on top.

Make a gravy by combining the pan drippings and giblet stock and following the instructions for gravy.
