Biscotti

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 3 Tbs oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ¾ tsp orange or almond extract
  • 1 2/3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp mace (optional)
  • ½ cup dried fruit (cherries or mixed tropical)

Method

Beat eggs until foamy. Add sugar and beat until pale. Add oil and extracts.

Sift flour with baking powder and mace. Beat into eggs. Stir in dried fruit.

Scrape dough onto greased sheet and form into a 9" × 2" shape using a spatula or brush dipped in milk. Bake @ 350° for about 25 minutes until golden. Cool 10 minutes. Reset oven to 325°.

Slice loaf on a bias and bake about 5 minutes per side (4 if baking on tiles) until golden.
