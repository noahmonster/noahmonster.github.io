Potato Corn Soup

Ingredients

    A
  • 2 onions, chopped
    B
  • 6 medium (4 large) potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 6 ½ cups milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • black pepper, ground
    C
  • 2 cups frozen corn

Method

Stir-fry or microwave A. Combine with B and simmer until tender, ~ 30 min. Partially mash. Add C.
